Rewards System: Explained

This is all about our reward system:

  • 7 days of healthy eating for whole class = going outside in one day for 15 minutes.
  • 14 days of healthy eating for whole class = free time on any day for 15 minutes.
  • 1 Month of healthy eating for whole class = Going outside any day for 30 minutes.
  • 2 months for healthy eating for whole class= Extra recess for 10 more minutes
  • 4 months of healthy eating for whole class = half of any day is tech day.
  • 6 months of healthy eating for whole class = free day.

These are our ideas. I hope you liked them.  BACK
