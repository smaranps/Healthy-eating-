Healthy Eating.

Healthy eating is when you start eating healthy and not eating any junk foods. Some healthy foods include vegetables like carrots and fruits like apples and bananas. Junk foods which are bad for your health include things like Burgers, pizzas, and chips. Junk foods and can only be eaten once in a while. A little too much and bad things might happen. So start eating healthy!
